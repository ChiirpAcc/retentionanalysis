Clean calculator logic
Win rate is not modeled from onboarding or free months because those terms are not cleanly observable for lost deals. The calculator starts with a base held-demo win-rate slider and then applies only the MRR effect.
Higher MRR lowers the adjusted win rate. Onboarding and free months affect churn, revenue timing, and cash, but not win rate.
What to use this for
Use this to compare deal structures directionally: onboarding %, free months, MRR, CAC threshold, breakeven month, and churn-adjusted revenue path.
The calculator is not a causal proof engine. It is a scenario model built around the best-controlled inputs we have.
