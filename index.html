Correct calculation flow
The calculator uses a fixed 100 held demos, predicts win rate from onboarding %, free months, MRR, leakage, and the selected month index, then converts that into expected won accounts. Only won accounts generate onboarding revenue, MRR, churn, and CAC recovery.
Example logic: 100 held demos × modeled win rate = expected wins. Those wins create onboarding cash immediately, then recurring revenue after free months, reduced over time by churn and leakage.
What executives should look at
The main outputs are:
• modeled win rate from 100 held demos
• expected won accounts
• expected customer churn by month
• cumulative cohort revenue
• CAC breakeven month
• how each deal variable changes win rate and churn
Win-rate caveat: the win-rate model is based on 12 monthly 2025 observations. Use it directionally, not as a precise causal forecast.
