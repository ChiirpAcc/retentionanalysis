Executive takeaways
⚖️

No single lever wins everywhere

The best commercial structure balances conversion, retention, and unit economics rather than maximizing one metric in isolation.

🔁

Interactions matter

Onboarding, free months, and MRR behave differently together than they do separately, so deals should be evaluated as packages.

🎯

Optimize net value

The recommended target is expected churn-adjusted net value per pipeline, with win rate and churn used as supporting guardrails.

Limitations: We observe final reported deal terms in HubSpot/source systems, not the full negotiation path, original anchor price, or all concessions offered during the sales process. Results are directional planning inputs, not precise causal rules for individual deals.
