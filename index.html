Clean calculator logic
Win rate is not modeled from MRR, onboarding, or free months. It is controlled by the base win-rate slider because lost deals do not have cleanly observable onboarding/free-month structures.
MRR affects revenue, cash per won account, cohort cash, breakeven, and LTV:CAC. Churn is modeled from onboarding % and free months.
What to use this for
Use this to compare deal structures directionally: onboarding %, free months, MRR, CAC threshold, breakeven month, and churn-adjusted revenue path.
The calculator is not a causal proof engine. It is a scenario model built around the best-controlled inputs we have.
