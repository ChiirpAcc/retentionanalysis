What this is not
Limits worth stating
  • Observational, not causal. Selection between deal types is not random; the coefficients are best controlled estimates given the history.
  • Win-rate model has 12 monthly observations. R² is high (0.69) but degrees of freedom are small. Use the manual override when the modelled rate looks implausible.
  • The 1-free-month bin dominates the data. 1,613 of 2,404 churn-eligible accounts. 0, 2, and 3 free-month bins are smaller.
  • CAC is a threshold, not a behavioural input. Higher CAC moves the breakeven line upward; it does not change predicted churn or LTV.
