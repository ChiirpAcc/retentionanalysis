Each band shows the modeled range of win rate and LTV:CAC across all deal structures in that churn tier. Low-churn structures occupy the top-right. High-churn structures cluster bottom-left — weaker on both economics and retention.

How to use this analysis: These models estimate directional patterns from historical data — use them to compare deal structures and guide pricing policy, not to predict any individual deal or account outcome. We observe final reported deal terms only, not the full negotiation context, so treat all outputs as planning inputs rather than precise causal rules.
